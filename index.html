Our Story

From a Home Kitchen
to Your Table

"Every dish we serve carries the memory of a kitchen that smelled of mustard seeds and turmeric — and a mother who cooked with nothing but love."

It started in 2019 with nothing more than a small home kitchen and an unshakeable love for Bengali breakfasts. Before the world knew what was coming, we were quietly serving hot luchi, dal, and fish curry to neighbours who kept coming back — not just for the food, but for the feeling of being home.

Then 2020 arrived, and everything changed. While the world stood still, we found our purpose. We pivoted — not out of desperation, but out of a deep desire to bring comfort to people who needed it most. Every parcel we packed was a small act of care. Every meal we delivered was a warm embrace in uncertain times.

By 2021, what began as a home passion had grown into a full cloud kitchen — and by 2024, Dakghar found its home in Noida's Sector 121, serving families and professionals craving the generational recipes and authentic flavours of Bengal. Every plate we serve is a letter from our roots — honest, warm, and made with love.
